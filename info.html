Did you know Orca are actually a member of the Dolphin family?


The dolphin family is diverse, with numerous species within the family Delphinidae. There are more than 40 species of dolphins, including the Bottlenose Dolphin, the Orca Whale, the Commerson's Dolphin, the Dusky Dolphin, and the Common Dolphin. Dolphins are known to be intelligent, social creatures that live in groups called pods. They use a complex communication system and exhibit a range of behaviors, including jumping and spinning out of the water, bow riding, and playing with objects such as seaweed or jellyfish. These playful creatures are found throughout the world's oceans and are loved by people all over the planet.
